PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement is entered into between Client and Service Provider.

SECTION 1 - LIABILITY
The liability clause states that damages are capped at $10,000. Neither party shall be liable 
for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from this agreement.

SECTION 2 - TERMINATION  
The notice period for termination is 30 days. Either party may terminate this agreement 
with written notice of 30 days to the other party. Upon termination, all outstanding 
payments become immediately due.

SECTION 3 - PAYMENT TERMS
Payment is due within 30 days of invoice. Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge.
The service provider shall submit invoices monthly for services rendered.

SECTION 4 - CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information shared 
during the course of this agreement. This obligation survives termination for 2 years.

SECTION 5 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work products created under this agreement remain the property of the client upon 
full payment. The service provider retains rights to general methodologies and tools.

SECTION 6 - DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
The arbitration shall take place in the jurisdiction of the client's principal place of business.
